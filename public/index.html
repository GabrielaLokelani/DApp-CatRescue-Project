Cat Rescue Shelter

Welcome to the "Cat Rescue" DApp. This distributed app runs on the Ethereum blockchain network and holds a shelter of cats as Solidity smart contract.
  • The shelter keeps images of each cat in the shelter along with their name, age, gender, short description, and their adoption availability.
  • Contract owner can submit new cats to be stored on the blockchain and in the shelter.
  • Users can view the existence of certain cats in the shelter.

Add a cat to the rescue shelter!

Contract owners can rescue (upload) new cats to the "Cat Rescue" smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain decentralized network.

Blockchain users can view cats in the shelter on the Ethereum network.

Adopt Cats
